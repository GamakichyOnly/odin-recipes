Fried Chicken Recipe

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 8 pieces of bone-in, skin-on chicken.
  • Buttermilk: 2 cups for marinating the chicken.
  • Flour: 2 cups for the breading.
  • Seasoning: 2 tsp each of salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs for the breading process.
  • Oil: Enough for deep frying, preferably vegetable or peanut oil.

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken: Soak the chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  2. Prepare the breading: In a large bowl, mix the flour with the seasonings. In another bowl, beat the eggs.
  3. Dredge the chicken: Dip each chicken piece into the egg, then into the flour mixture, ensuring it's well coated.
  4. Fry the chicken: Heat the oil in a deep-fryer or large skillet to 350°F (175°C). Fry chicken pieces until golden brown and juices run clear, turning occasionally to brown evenly.
  5. Drain: Place fried chicken on a plate lined with paper towels to remove excess oil. Serve while hot.
