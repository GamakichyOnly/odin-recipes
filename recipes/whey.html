Whey Protein Smoothie Recipe

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Whey Protein: 1 scoop (choose your preferred flavor, e.g., vanilla or chocolate).
  • Banana: 1 ripe banana.
  • Almond Milk: 1 cup (or any milk of your choice).
  • Peanut Butter: 1 tablespoon (optional for extra protein and flavor).
  • Spinach: A handful (for added nutrients and a green boost).
  • Ice cubes: A handful (for a cooler drink).
  • Chia Seeds: 1 teaspoon (optional for added fiber and nutrition).

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients: Ensure all ingredients are at hand and the banana is peeled.
  2. Add to blender: Start with the almond milk, then add the banana, whey protein, peanut butter, spinach, ice cubes, and chia seeds.
  3. Blend: Blend on high until the mixture is smooth. Ensure there are no lumps and everything is well combined.
  4. Adjust consistency: If the smoothie is too thick, you can add more almond milk to achieve your desired consistency.
  5. Serve: Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately.
